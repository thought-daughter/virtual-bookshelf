✨ How to Export Your Goodreads Library ✨

To populate your virtual bookshelf, you need to upload your Goodreads data file. Follow these steps on a browser or computer (the export option is not available in the Goodreads mobile app):

  1. Go to goodreads.com and log in.Note: If you are using a phone, hold the link and select "open in new tab."
  2. Click on "My Books" in the top navigation bar.
  3. On the left-hand sidebar, look under "Tools" and click "Import and Export".Note: If you are using a phone, hit the "..." or "settings" and select "Request Desktop Site."
  4. Click the "Export Library" button in the top left.
  5. Once a link appears saying "Your export from (date - time)", click it to download your .csv file.
  6. Return to this app, click "Choose File", and select your freshly downloaded file!
