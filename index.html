✨ How to Export Your Goodreads Library ✨

To populate your virtual bookshelf, you need to upload your Goodreads data file. Follow these steps on a browser or computer (the export option is not available in the Goodreads mobile app):

  1. Log In: Go to goodreads.com and log in.
  2. Navigate to My Books: Click on "My Books" in the top navigation bar.
  3. Find the Import/Export Tool: On the left-hand sidebar, look under "Tools" and click "Import and Export".
  4. Generate Your Export: Click the "Export Library" button in the top left.
    Note: If you have a large library, it might take a minute or two.
  5. Download Your File: Once a link appears saying "Your export from (date - time)", click it to download your .csv file.
  6. Upload Here: Return to this app, click "Choose File", and select your freshly downloaded file!
